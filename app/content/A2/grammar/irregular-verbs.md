# Common Irregular Verbs (A2)

At the A2 level, you should know the past forms of these frequently used verbs. Remember, irregular verbs do not end in **-ed**!

## Three Groups of Irregular Verbs

To make them easier to learn, we can group them by pattern:

### Group 1: No Change (Cut - Cut - Cut)

These verbs are the easiest! The form is the same for Present, Past, and Participle.

| Infinitive | Past Simple | Past Participle | Meaning                           |
| :--------- | :---------- | :-------------- | :-------------------------------- |
| **Cost**   | Cost        | Cost            | Costar                            |
| **Cut**    | Cut         | Cut             | Cortar                            |
| **Hit**    | Hit         | Hit             | Golpear                           |
| **Hurt**   | Hurt        | Hurt            | Herir/Doler                       |
| **Put**    | Put         | Put             | Poner                             |
| **Read**   | Read\*      | Read\*          | Leer (\*Pronounced 'red' in past) |
| **Shut**   | Shut        | Shut            | Cerrar                            |
