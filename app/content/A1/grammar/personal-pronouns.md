## ðŸ’¡ Important Tips

1. **"I" is always capitalized.**
    - _Example:_ Am **I** late? (NOT: Am i late?)

2. **"You" is the same for singular and plural.**
    - The context tells you if it is one person or many.
    - _El contexto te dice si es una persona o muchas._

3. **"It" is for things and animals.**
    - But if a pet has a name, we often use **He** or **She**.
    - _Pero si una mascota tiene nombre, a menudo usamos **He** o **She**._
