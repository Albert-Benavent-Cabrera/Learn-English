# Describing People

In this section, we will learn how to describe a person's physical appearance and ask related questions.

## 1. General Questions

Before describing ourselves, we need to know the most common questions:

- **Question:** What do you look like?
- **Answer:** I am tall and I have brown hair.

For formal situations:

- **Question:** Could you describe yourself, please?
- **Answer:** Certainly, I am of medium height...

To ask about specific details:

- **Question:** How tall are you?
- **Question:** What is your hair like?

## 2. Age (La Edad)

- **Question:** How old are you?
- **Answer:** I am 25 years old.

Common expressions:

- **"I'm a teenager"** - Soy un adolescente
- **"I'm twentyish"** - Tengo veintipico
- **"I'm young"** - Soy joven
- **"I'm middle-aged"** - Eres de mediana edad
- **"I'm elderly"** - Soy una persona mayor
- **"I'm in my early forties"** - Tengo cuarenta y pocos
- **"I'm in my late forties"** - Tengo cuarenta y muchos

## 3. Height (La Altura)

- **Question:** How tall are you?
- **Answer:** I am 1.75 meters tall.

Expressions:

- **"I'm very tall"** - Soy muy alto
- **"I'm of average height"** - Soy de estatura media
- **"I'm short"** - Soy bajo
- **"I'm roughly 6 feet tall"** - Mido casi 1 metro 80
- **"I'm quite short"** - Soy bastante bajo

## 4. Weight (El Peso)

**Note:** Be careful, asking about weight can be sensitive.

- **Question:** What do you weigh?
- **Answer:** I weigh 70 kilos.

Expressions:

- **"I'm slim"** - Estoy delgado
- **"I'm fit"** - Estoy en forma
- **"I'm athletic"** - Soy de complexión atlética
- **"I'm well-built"** - De complexión fuerte
- **"I'm chubby"** - Gordito (más amable que fat)
- **"I'm average weight"** - Peso normal

## 5. The Face (La Cara)

- **"I have a round face"** - Tengo la cara redonda
- **"I have an oval face"** - Tengo la cara ovalada
- **"I have a square face"** - Tengo la cara cuadrada
- **"I have a thin face"** - Tengo la cara fina
- **"I have freckles"** - Tengo pecas
- **"I have a scar"** - Tengo una cicatriz

## 6. The Nose (La Nariz)

- **"I have a big nose"** - Tengo la nariz grande
- **"I have a small nose"** - Tengo la nariz pequeña
- **"I have a pointed nose"** - Tengo la nariz puntiaguda
- **"I have a hooked nose"** - Tengo la nariz ganchuda

## 7. The Hair (El Pelo)

Order of adjectives: **Length + Shape + Color**

> **Formula:** I have + (Size) + (Shape) + (Color) + hair

Examples:

- **"I have long curly blond hair"** - Tengo el pelo largo, rizado y rubio
- **"I have short straight dark hair"** - Tengo el pelo corto, liso y oscuro
- **"I have wavy brown hair"** - Tengo el pelo ondulado y castaño
- **"I have shoulder-length hair"** - Tengo el pelo por los hombros

## 8. The Eyes (Los Ojos)

Order of adjectives: **Size + Shape + Color**

> **Formula:** I have + (Size) + (Shape) + (Color) + eyes

Examples:

- **"I have big blue eyes"** - Tengo ojos grandes y azules
- **"I have small dark eyes"** - Tengo ojos pequeños y oscuros
- **"I have round green eyes"** - Tengo ojos redondos y verdes
