## Going - Yendo / Ir a

- **Meaning 1 (Action):** The present participle of "go" (moving from one place to another).
    - **Spanish:** Yendo.
    - **Example:** _"I am **going** to the gym."_
    - **Spanish Translation:** Estoy yendo al gimnasio.
- **Meaning 2 (Future Plans):** Part of the structure "be going to" to express future intentions.
    - **Spanish:** Ir a (hacer algo).
    - **Example:** _"I am **going** to build a new robot."_
    - **Spanish Translation:** Voy a construir un nuevo robot.
