This video covers the essential grammar points for the B2 (Upper Intermediate) level:

### 1. Conditionals

- **Zero, First, Second, Third, and Mixed Conditionals.**
- Focus on the structure and when to use each (real vs. hypothetical, past vs. present).

### 2. Wishes / If Only

- Used to express regrets or desire for things to be different.
- **Wish + Past Simple** (present desire).
- **Wish + Past Perfect** (past regret).
- **Wish + Would** (annoying behavior).

### 3. Modal Verbs for Deduction

- **Must / Can't / Might / Could / May.**
- Used to express how certain we are about something in the present or past.

### 4. Causatives

- **Have / Get something done.**
- Used when someone else does something for us (e.g., "I had my hair cut").

### 5. Passive Voice

- Focusing on the action rather than the doer.
- Structure: **Subject + be + past participle.**

### 6. ING / TO Expressions

- Verb patterns (gerunds vs. infinitives).
- Verbs that change meaning depending on whether they are followed by -ing or to- (e.g., remember, stop, try).
